TITAN DOSSIER

The Titans are colossal, ancient creatures that have roamed Earth long before humanity‚Äôs rise to power. Thought to be myths or legends for centuries, these beings represent a primordial force of nature, with the power to reshape the planet's environment.

From Godzilla, the King of the Monsters, to Kong, the protector of Skull Island, their actions are driven by instincts tied to Earth's natural balance. Scientists believe Titans are key players in an ecological cycle predating human civilization.

MONARCH SCIENCES

Monarch Sciences is the global organization tasked with studying and monitoring Titans. Established in secrecy, Monarch operates facilities worldwide to track Titan activity and mitigate threats.

Their mission: to protect humanity while unlocking the secrets of these creatures. Despite public skepticism, Monarch remains the only line of defense against catastrophic Titan encounters.

APEX CYBERNETICS

Apex Cybernetics operates in opposition to Monarch, viewing Titans as resources to exploit. Their creation of Mechagodzilla represents humanity's dangerous ambition to control these forces of nature.

Apex's experiments threaten the delicate balance between humans and Titans, making them Monarch's greatest adversary.

Who Are The Iwi?

The Iwi are a secretive and spiritual indigenous tribe native to Skull Island. For generations, they lived in harmony with the island's dangerous ecosystem, including titanic creatures that would devastate other civilizations.

Culture & Beliefs

The Iwi practiced a spiritual way of life, believing Kong was a living god - the guardian of the island and protector of balance.

  • Art & Symbolism: Ancient murals depicting Titan battles
  • Silence & Discipline: Non-verbal communication traditions
  • Sacred Rituals: Ceremonies honoring Kong's protection

The Fall of Skull Island

Following Kong's departure and the intensification of Skull Island's storms, the island became uninhabitable. The surviving Iwi were rescued by Monarch.
